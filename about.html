Our Story

Committed to Excellence

Founded in 2014, Kaptan started with a simple mission: to provide the safest, most comfortable, and reliable transfer services in Istanbul. What began as a small fleet of vehicles has grown into one of the city's most trusted transfer companies.

We understand that your journey doesn't start when you reach your destination—it begins the moment you book with us. That's why we've built our service around three core values: safety, comfort, and reliability.

Today, we serve thousands of travelers every month, from business professionals to families on vacation, ensuring each one arrives safely and comfortably at their destination.
